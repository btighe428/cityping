**Information Hierarchy:**

| Priority | Section | Content | Max Length |
|----------|---------|---------|------------|

| 2 | **Action Required** | Specific instruction + deadline | 1-2 lines |

## What to Exclude at 9am

### âŒ Don't Include

| Content Type | Why Not | When It Belongs |
|--------------|---------|-----------------|
| **Long news articles** | No time to read | Noon email |
| **Restaurant recommendations** | Wrong context | 7pm email |
| **Event listings** | Too far ahead | Noon or 7pm |
| **Yesterday's recap** | Old news | Monthly recap |
| **Multiple emojis** | Visual clutter | None (or 7pm) |
| **Marketing/upgrade prompts** | Interrupts task | Welcome flow |
| **Detailed weather forecast** | Too much info | Weather section, max 2 lines |
| **Full transit schedule** | Overwhelming | Link out only |
| **Parking statistics/facts** | Not actionable | Monthly email |
| **"Sleep in!" messaging** | Too casual | Never (per content standards) |

## Tone Considerations for 9am

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | 9am Application | Example |
|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Direct** | Lead with the answer | "ASP suspended" not "Good morning!" |
| **Calm** | Reduce anxiety, not add to it | "No need to move your car" not "You're safe!" |
| **Professional** | Respect their time pressure | No exclamation points, no emojis in subject |
| **Useful** | Every word earns its place | Context only when it changes behavior |
| **Confident** | Clear authority on parking | Not "we think" or "probably" |

### 9am vs Other Slots

| Element | 9am (Parking Brief) | Noon (City Pulse) | 7pm (Evening Digest) |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| **Primary Goal** | Answer "What about my car?" | Update on city | Plan tomorrow/leisure |
| **Urgency** | High (time-sensitive) | Medium | Low |
| **Length** | Short (<150 words) | Medium (~400 words) | Longer (~600 words) |
| **Tone** | Efficient, calm | Engaging, informative | Relaxed, exploratory |
| **Emojis** | 0-1 (weather only) | 2-3 | 3-4 |
| **Subject Style** | "Parking Brief â€” [Date]: [Status]" | "NYC Today: [Hook]" | "Evening Edition: [Teaser]" |

## Content Validation Checklist

Before sending any 9am email, verify:

- [ ] **Status is clear within 3 seconds of opening**
- [ ] **Action is stated explicitly** (not implied)
- [ ] **Time deadlines are specific** (not "this morning")
- [ ] **Weather info is relevant to parking/driving**
- [ ] **Transit info only included if it affects parking decisions**
- [ ] **No more than 1 emoji total**
- [ ] **Subject line follows format: "Parking Brief â€” [Date]: [Status]"**
- [ ] **No exclamation points** (calm, not urgent)
- [ ] **Total email <150 words**
- [ ] **Nothing requires scrolling on mobile**
